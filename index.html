For Labs: No More Staffing Panic

When a technician calls in sick, quits suddenly, or workload spikes, your lab still has to deliver. Lab Crew gives you a dependable way to plug that gap with verified, skill-matched technicians.

The Problem

Labs across India face repeated short-term shortages: last-minute sick leave, sudden resignations, seasonal test spikes and machine-specific skill gaps.

  • Delayed reports and turn-around time issues
  • Stressed, overworked core team
  • Informal, unverified temporary staff
  • Quality and compliance risks

The Solution

A simple way to request a technician for a day, a week or a month, with transparent skill matching and one consolidated invoice.

  • Short-term cover from 1 day to 30 days
  • Technicians matched by department and instruments
  • Verified profiles and ratings from other labs
  • Lab Crew handles payouts and documentation

How It Works for Labs

Step 1

Tell us what you need

Share your dates, shift timings, department and instrument needs. It takes less than a minute.

Step 2

We match a technician

We match you with verified technicians based on skills, experience, rating and distance to your lab.

Step 3

Shift completed, one invoice

Technician attends the shift, you confirm attendance, and pay Lab Crew. We handle payouts to the technician.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you provide a technician?

During the pilot, this will depend on the city and the type of technician you need. The long-term goal is to match labs within a few hours in major cities.

Which cities are you focusing on first?

We are starting with New Delhi, Bengaluru and Hyderabad, and will expand based on demand from labs and technicians.

Do technicians pay any registration fee?

No. During the early stages, registration for technicians is free.

Can we also hire full-time technicians through Lab Crew?

Yes. Short-term cover is the priority, but we can also support full-time hiring and will share more when we speak with you.
